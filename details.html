The city of Woodstock, NY invites you to a FREE open concert series, SNHU-a-palooza. The concerts will run on the first Friday of every month in Robinson Farms field.

REGISTRATION is required and Admission is free. Attendnce is limited to 50,000 people and each guest can only attend one time.

The SNHU-a-palooze concert is set to kick off Thursday, June 5th at 4:00pm and will continue on the first Friday of every month for 4 performances. The last concert will be September 4, 2020. Each three hour concert will feature a mixture of classic rock, grunge, urban, and local hip-hop. There will be a total of 4 bands performing each day. Following the concert the audience will be treated to free watermelon and ice cream.
